Techology is becoming ever more accessible for both use & development, so there is no better time than the present to use our massive problem solving potential on a local level, cultivating organic seeds that will emanate through their real impact, instead of vacuous attentional based marketing that has become a corner-stone of corporate success.My mission is to use the breadth of my experience to create paradigm breaking tools to solve various local problems in order to help shift our collective mental models of the potential of emerging open-source technologies. The two spaces I am particuarly interested in developing are blockchain and aug-mented reality/mixed reality (AR/MR). Blockchain can be utilized in commu-nities to level hierarchies through its decentralization, while also recogniz-ing & encouraging engagement through tokenization that is not based on the current speculative mess that cryptocurrencies have produced. I am currently working on a project that uses simple bonding curves & non-fungible tokens to achieve this on the Ethereum blockchain-- in essence the value is built by the community itself, & block-chain methodology simplifies the pro-cess of allowing contributing members to garner recognition for their real effort. AR/MR can be harnessed to provide engaging insights with image tracking & location based markers that elucidate information about one’s sur-roundings. Instead of using devices to escape the present, it becomes a tool for engaging with old & new enviorn-ment with ultimate connectivity.
